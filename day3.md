Built something fun this weekend: an AI agent that writes poems about people 😄
Not a "productivity hack." Not a "game-changer." Just a genuinely fun little automation I built in n8n because I wanted to see how far I could push it.
Here's what it does:
📝 Someone fills out a form (name, looks, profession)
 🔀 A Switch node routes them based on their job — Video Editor, Graphic Designer, or AI Manager
 🤖 An AI Agent (powered by Google Gemini) writes a custom rated poem about them
 ✅ Everything gets logged straight into a table — name, profession, status, rating, and the poem itself
Ran my first test on my friend Harshit — "tall guy, fashion freak, AI Manager." The agent rated him ⭐ and wrote him a poem. He was equal parts amused and confused, which is exactly the reaction I was going for.
The real win here wasn't the poem. It was watching the whole pipeline — form → routing logic → AI generation → database update — run end to end without me touching anything after hitting submit.
n8n keeps surprising me with how much you can build without writing a single line of backend code. Next up: making the routing logic smarter and adding a few more "professions" to the mix.
